Italian Chicken Salad

Rp. 20k

Estimate Price

Description

Salad is a healthy and refreshing meal that is rich in vitamins, minerals, and fiber. It is also low in calories and fat, making it a great choice for a healthy diet.

Ingredients

  1. 50gr Lettuce
  2. 10gr Purple cabbage
  3. 10gr Cucumber
  4. 10gr Tomato
  5. 10gr Edamame
  6. 100gr Grilled chicken
  7. 20gr Boiled Corn

Directions

  1. Wash and chop lettuce, purple cabbage, and cucumber.
  2. Boil eggs and corn separately.
  3. Grill chicken until cooked.
  4. Peel and slice boiled eggs.
  5. Cut grilled chicken into bite-sized pieces.
